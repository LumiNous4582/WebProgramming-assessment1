Explore the Vast Universe



Honkai: Star Rail is a turn-based sci-fi RPG developed by HoYoverse. Set in a universe where myth and technology collide, players board the Astral Express, a cosmic train that travels across galaxies and mysterious worlds. As the Trailblazer, you uncover secrets behind powerful entities called Aeons, each representing different ideologies, while forming bonds with a diverse cast of companions. The game features cinematic storytelling, deep character customization, and a blend of traditional RPG systems with a modern, stylized presentation.

Gameplay

Combat

Turn-Based Combat Each character takes turns based on their speed stat. Combat involves using Basic Attacks, Skills, and powerful Ultimates, which can be triggered even outside of a character's turn for tactical depth.

Exploration & Puzzles

Outside combat, players explore vibrant planets, solve environmental puzzles, and take part in events and minigames that expand the lore and rewards.

Simulated Universe & Forgotten Hall

Special game modes like Simulated Universe (a roguelike challenge) and Forgotten Hall (a trial tower) add replayability and rewards for strategic mastery.
