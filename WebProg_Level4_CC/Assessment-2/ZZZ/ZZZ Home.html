Explore New Eridu



Zenless Zone Zero is a free-to-play action RPG developed by HoYoverse, the creators of Genshin Impact and Honkai: Star Rail. Released on July 4, 2024, for PlayStation 5, PC, iOS, and Android, with an Xbox Series X|S version slated for June 2025, ZZZ transports players to New Eridu, the last bastion of civilization in a world ravaged by supernatural disasters known as "Hollows." These anomalies spawn entities called "Ethereals," posing threats that players, acting as "Proxies," must navigate and combat.

Gameplay

Combat

ZZZ features dynamic real-time battles where players can chain together flashy combos, dodge with precise timing, and switch between characters mid-fight for devastating follow-up attacks. Each character has a unique combat style, elemental attribute, and special ability, encouraging team synergy and tactical play.

Hollow Exploration

The game takes place in an alternate dimension called the Hollow ‚Äî unstable zones full of Etheric energy, enemies, and hidden lore. Players navigate these zones through roguelike-inspired map segments, selecting encounters, events, and upgrades as they delve deeper.

Upgrade & Cusomization

Agents can be enhanced through leveling up, equipping W-Engines (weapons), and installing Drive Discs (passive boosts). This allows players to personalize their playstyle and build teams tailored to their preferences.
