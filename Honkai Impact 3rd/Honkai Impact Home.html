Unravel the Truth Behind the Honkai



Honkai Impact 3rd is a fast-paced action RPG developed by HoYoverse (formerly miHoYo). Set in a world plagued by a mysterious force known as the Honkai, players command a squad of powerful warriors called Valkyries to fight against apocalyptic threats, corrupted creatures, and twisted versions of humanity. Blending anime aesthetics, cinematic storytelling, and fluid combat, the game follows central characters like Kiana, Mei, and Bronya across multiple timelines and universes. What begins as a simple fight against evil evolves into a tragic and profound narrative about sacrifice, identity, and hope.

Gameplay

Combat

Players build a team of 3 Valkyries and can switch between them mid-combat to chain abilities and perform QTEs (Quick Time Entrances), triggering powerful team synergies.

Post-Honkai Odyssey

An open-world 3D expansion featuring different mechanics, exploring a post-cataclysm Earth with older versions of the characters.

Gamemodes

Story Campaign: Packed with animated cutscenes and deep lore
Elysian Realm: A roguelike mode with buffs and team experiments
Abyss & Memorial Arena: High-end challenges with ranking and score chasing
Co-op Raids: Real-time multiplayer against giant bosses
